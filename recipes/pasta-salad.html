Pesto Pasta Salad

  • 1 lb. short pasta, such as fusilli or penne
  • 1/4 c. pine nuts
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 c. basil leaves
  • 1/4 c. finely grated Parmesan, plus more for serving
  • 1/4 c. extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tsp. lemon juice
  • 1 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1 pt. grape or cherry tomatoes, halved lengthwise
